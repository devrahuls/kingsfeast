What makes us different?

Kingsfeast began with a simple vision—bringing joy through delicious and affordable ice cream. Over the time, our commitment to taste and quality has helped us grow into a trusted brand, reaching 500+ retailers and 10+ distributors across 10 cities, including Bokaro, Hazaribagh, and beyond. Our signature Milky Pop has become a favorite, loved for its rich flavor and creamy texture. As we expand, we remain dedicated to delivering delicious frozen treats that create moments of happiness for families and friends.

What Drives Us?

At Kingsfeast, our mission is to deliver high-quality, creamy Milky Pop ice cream that brings joy to every customer. We believe in using the finest ingredients to create flavors that delight, ensuring every bite is rich, smooth, and satisfying. Our goal is to make Milky Pop a household favorite, spreading happiness across cities with every bite. Looking ahead, we aim to expand to more regions,reaching 1,000+ retailers and 25+ distributors, while maintaining our commitment to quality and affordability. We also strive to introduce new flavors and innovations, ensuring our customers always have something exciting to enjoy. Kingsfeast is more than just ice cream—its a celebration of taste, happiness, and togetherness.
